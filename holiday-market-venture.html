Holiday Market Sales Venture

September - November 2019

Organized and ran a pop-up shop at a local holiday market to sell handmade jewelery

Objective

  • Organize inventory and secure a space within two months to sell handmade seed-bead bracelets and keychains at a local market
  • Gain experience in small-scale entrepreneurship, standardized production, and customer interaction

Process

  • Selected and created bead patterns and quantities of each product to sell
  • Determined pricing, inventory, and product packaging
  • Coordinated with market organizers for booth space and setup
  • Engaged with customers and managed sales

Results

  • Developed practical skills in entrepreneurship, customer service, and standardized product manufacturing practices
  • Learned how to price products effectively, evaluating costs of production and desired profit
  • Sold out of select styles
