Mechanical Team

Formula Snail

October - November 2025

A roller-coaster-style toy inspired by Formula racing and the film Turbo, with mechanical design inspiration from retractable tape measures and Hot Wheels toys.

AutoCAD SOLIDWORKS 3D-Printing MS Excel/Word

Objective

  • Create a children’s toy as a final project for a first-year mechanical engineering course
  • Apply the engineering design process and prototyping methods to evaluate mechanical feasibility
  • Design a non-motorized propulsion mechanism that propels a snail-shaped figurine on wheels along a modular track
  • Ensure the toy is both engaging and safe for children ages 6+

Process

  • Brainstormed and evaluated multiple toy concepts as a team to select the optimal design for prototyping
  • Expanded SOLIDWORKS and AutoCAD skills to model components, test integration and support rapid iteration
  • Coordinated weekly project goals using Excel, strengthening communication and collaboration within the team
  • Conducted research for solutions to each function, considering feasibility and relevant Canadian Toy Regulations

Results

  • The fastest snail on wheels!
  • A 3D-printed toy snail that is propelled along a custom-built track using a torsion spring mechanism at an exciting but safe speed
  • Developed a modular track system that can be configured in a variety of ways via universal connectors and purposefully engineered track pieces
  • Optimized track design that enabled smooth, reliable motion along both curved and straight components
  • Play the video to see the toy in action!
