About Me

Hi! My name is Jumaana, and I am a first-year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a strong interest in mechanical design and product development. I enjoy transforming simple concept sketches into detailed 3D models and functional prototypes that bring ideas to life. Through both individual and team projects, I have gained experience in the product development cycle. From ideation to design and prototyping, I developed proficiency in tools such as SOLIDWORKS and 3D printing (FDM).

Beyond engineering, my experience running a pop-up shop led me to discover my passion for entrepreneurship. I aspire to lead my own start-up, combining creativity and engineering to build innovative products that can improve my community.

I am currently searching for Fall 2026 co-op opportunities. Feel free to view my resume, check out some of my featured projects, or reach out to discuss how I can contribute to your team!
