LEGO Goose Piece Design

March - April 2026

A custom-designed, LEGO-compatible goose element inspired by Canadian Geese and designed with a focus on stylization, playability, and integration into the LEGO System in Play.

Autodesk Fusion 360 SOLIDWORKS 3D-Printing Sketching

Objective

  • Design and prototype a custom LEGO element piece in the shape of a Canadian Goose that successfully integrates into the LEGO System in Play
  • Apply iterative prototyping to evaluate design compatibility and refine connection interface, ensuring the piece securely attaches to standard LEGO pieces
  • Explore the design process and constraints of creating a custom LEGO element, including geometric compatibility, structural integrity, and aesthetic appeal

Process

Step 1: Developing a connection interface

  • Tested connection interface by modelling a 2 x 1 stud flat LEGO piece in Autodesk Fusion 360 and 3D-printing multiple prototypes
  • Modified interface dimensions by 0.1 mm increments to achieve sufficient clutch power with standard LEGO pieces

Step 2: Initial element concept design

  • Developed early sketches of concept designs, taking inspiration from real geese and visual representations such as plush toys and cartoons to explore different stylization approaches

Step 3: CAD modelling and rapid prototyping

  • Modelled the element in SOLIDWORKS, using spline- and ellipse-based sketches, lofts with guide curves, and fillets to achieve organic geometry
  • 3D printed multiple design iterations to optimize body geometry and size integration with LEGO pieces, including side-by-side minifigure placement and repeatable use

Results

  • A LEGO-style Canadian Goose piece to add to my minifigure collection!
  • Successfuly achieved secure connection to standard LEGO pieces while maintaining stylized, organic geometry that captures the essence of a goose in a playful way
  • Appropriate scale and size for integration into the LEGO System in Play, allowing for side-by-side placement with minifigures and repeatable use
Key Specifications
Height 23.9 mm
Width 9.7 mm
Connection size 2 x 1 studs
Overall length 4 studs including beak and tail overhang
Prototype 3D-printed PLA
